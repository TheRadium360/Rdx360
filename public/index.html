About RDX360.Network
RDX360.NETWORK is a special variant of bitcoin blockchain that utilizes a Layer 2 extensibility engine to enable leveraged transactions while also lowering transaction fees. RDX360 is the RDX360.NETWORK governance token, with a total quantity of 10,000,000. RDX360 is traded on the exchanges Hotbit, Binance, and Coinbase. The cost of listing RDX360 is anticipated to be $30.
Pre-sale Airdrop

How Does RDX.360.NETWORK Work?

RDX360.NETWORK collaborated with Radium, which utilized Radium, the Layer two scalability system which will improve the trading on the platform. In simple terms, the impact is similar to the future Eth 2.0 upgrade since the cost of gas will be reduced to zero, minimum sizes for trades will be decreased, and the trading fees will be less.

The goal of RDX360.NETWORK is to provide secure trading solutions with cheap gas and charge costs.. To achieve this, the platform is moving toward Layer 2 with the help of Radium to boost the capacity of its trade settlement.
